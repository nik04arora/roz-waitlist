What is Roz?

Roz is an investing app built for people who want to build wealth without having to figure out mutual fund investing alone. You choose how much to invest each month. Roz experts build a mutual fund portfolio around your goals, automate your investments, and help you stay on track when markets fall.

Why should I use Roz instead of investing on my own?

Investing on your own may work if you are comfortable researching funds, deciding what fits your goals, and staying disciplined when markets move. Roz is for people who do not want to make those decisions alone.

Our experts build your mutual fund portfolio around your goals, explain what you own and why, and give you support when staying invested feels difficult.

How much does Roz cost?

Roz does not charge you a separate subscription or advisory fee. Roz is powered by Abacus, an AMFI-registered Mutual Fund Distributor. When you invest through mutual funds distributed by Abacus, Abacus receives commission from mutual fund houses through the fund's expense ratio.

Does Roz use regular or direct mutual fund plans?

Roz invests through regular mutual fund plans. Regular plans include distributor commission within the fund's expense ratio, which is how Abacus is compensated for providing expert-led portfolio support through Roz.
